ACF Merchandise Promotion

OVERVIEW: Asian Christian Fellowship (ACF) is one of the Christian faith clubs at Western University. Every year, they design merch themed around a particular Bible verse for its members to purchase.

TASK: Inspired by a Christian clothing brand, The Boy With Style, the ACF graphics team wanted an illustrated piece that would help promote the club’s 2020-2021 merchandise.

PLAN: Work together with the merch design team to create an illustration that shows off the merch in a flattering way.

Process

1. Ideation

Since the merch designs were already finalized, I based the palette around the orange and black colour scheme. Since our original inspiration stems from The Boy With Style’s own promotional efforts, the composition for this illustration was also based on their campaign.

2. Sketch

I wanted the overall feel of the piece to be relaxed, so the poses are more akin with “standing around” as opposed to “modeling”. There’s an inside joke about chelsea boots within the fellowship, so I though it’d be a nice detail to include in the piece. I opted for the standing figure to sport the hoodie and the front of the design, since the hood would’ve partially covered the back design and would be frustrating to look at as a close-up.

Final Illustration

This piece was so much fun to illustrate-- I learned a lot about how clothes drape over the body, and how light behaves as it hits the clothing folds. It is currently one of my favorite illustrations, and it was the most challenging to complete. The ACF exec team loved how it turned out, and it received 32 likes on the group’s Facebook page!
